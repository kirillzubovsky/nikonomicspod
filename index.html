About the pod

I started Nikonomics to focus on the economics of small business. We delve into the dynamic world of entrepreneurship, offering listeners a deep dive into the mechanics, strategies and stories behind successful ventures with the founders themselves!

Each episode aims to educate on different business models, offer practical advice, and provide inspirational anecdotes to help expand the definition of "what is possible" for business owners and aspiring entrepreneurs.

Who's Running This Operation?

Hey! I'm Nik Hulewsky. I'm a seasoned entrepreneur and trusted advisor, who loves problem solving, helping people and having fun. I've owned and operated over a dozen companies across multiple industries, including healthcare, e-commerce, and crypto.

I've had multiple 7-figure exits—and, in the spirit of full transparency, an equal number of 7-figure lessons learned (losses). I have an undergraduate degree from the University of Southern California in Economics and an MBA from UCLA.

I have been married for almost 16 years and have 4 boys at home ages 10, 7, 4 and 2.
